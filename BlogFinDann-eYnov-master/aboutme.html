Antoine MACHEDA

My desire to work in the digital world began during my high school years by discovering a passion for video games. This passion allowed me to have a first opinion on teamwork and the cohesion that goes with it. This also allowed me to pass a second barrier, that of language. Indeed, the communities in the video games are not only French-speaking and an adaptation is necessary in particular in English in order to allow a total understanding between each one. It was after a project for the baccalaureate that I discovered a second passion for IT development. Having chosen the computer science and digital science specialty for the Bac. The final assessment was on the creation of a game / software and its presentation to a jury, all in a group. We therefore created our first Python game inspired by the famous Plague Inc game, an epidemic simulator on a population represented by colored cells. It is therefore with will and determination that I joined the Ynov campus in order to carry out my studies in an environment that suits me. I had a new opportunity to work with different groups on several projects. Thanks to these many experiences, team cohesion, group work with concessions and respect for each other's ideas have become a real value in the common projects that I undertake.

In addition to my studies, I work in the catering industry in a fast food chain, Subway. This new experience in an open environment is far from my work habits, alone behind my screen or in a group behind our screens. The service required in front of the customers or the rules within the premises of the restaurant as well as the respect of the schedules allowed me to acquire new practices. Within the restaurant, punctuality, politeness and respect towards the customer as well as towards his teammates or his superiors are golden rules that I fully share
