Want to travel? TravelHome !

  • August 16, 2020
  • By Antoine MACHEDA

When traveling, do you prefer comfort and services in a good hotel, or rather freedom and home stay with a private rental? Two schools ensuring a good vacation! Nowadays there are many sites for renting goods between individuals, whether it is AirBnB or Booking.com, there are many choices. During my apprenticeship at Campus Ynov, one of our project was to create our own travel site for individuals, in a style similar to AirBnB. I will share the design of the site with you in great detail.

Project et possibilities

Designing an entire site is no easy task when you're just starting out. There are different possibilities available to us: use a site editor by filling in only its content without worrying about writing the code. Conversely, we can create the code from A to Z or use existing templates named Template. For the creation of this one, our team chose to use this Template format. The functionalities we were looking for had to be similar to those of the market already in place, namely the possibility of finding accommodation in the city of their choice, of choosing between the various offers of private listings for rent. and finally to book for yourself and your friends or family at the right dates. I will share the detailed design with you as well as the various milestones that marked this project soon after. Behind these foods, the "sandwich artists", the honorary title given to restaurant staff.

Between conception and realisation

The biggest fear of developers is facing a blank sheet. Computer development is a lot like being a writer. You have to know how to find the words to best express your idea or even represent it. The first few weeks of work, my colleague and I focused on the visual aspect of our site. It was with the help of design that the idea we had took shape: pair this mockup with a Template. The main idea was there. We then sought to implement the features we wanted in the site, like those described above. It is thanks to the different languages ​​of the web that this project has become a reality. From HTML, CSS or JS to PHP, our site took shape week after week and at the end of the year, TravelHome was born. Although this project may have been successful, it has however not been uploaded and is currently not visible on the internet.

So if you want to go on a trip, look for us, you will be won over!

Posted by Antoine Macheda

For having used rental sites between individuals many times during my travels, the project to design one was quite stimulating and interesting. Knowing what was behind the beautiful photos and advertisements posted was a real opportunity to learn more about an area that I like : travel. It was a great opportunity to learn more about the languages ​​of the web. This experience will remain one of my favorites in the context of Web development, especially thanks to the pride felt during its final visualization.
